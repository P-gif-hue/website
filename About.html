A GUIDE TO OUR WEDDING WEEK IN CANCUN

A Gentle Note for Our Guests

We can’t wait to celebrate together.

  • Upon arrival you will be told the timings for each event alongside the locations. Please allow time to arrive to each event promptly — the resort is quite large.
  • During wedding ceremonies, we ask you to remain seated, it is really important to us that our ceremonies will be silent.
  • During both ceremonies we ask children to also be supervised and be seated the whole time.
  • The Vidhi ceremony is an early start, we understand if guests would like to join a bit later for the haldi.
  • Finally we ask for our faces to be kept off all social media
